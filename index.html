About Me

Hello! I'm Justin, recent graduate of General Assembly SF in Full Stack Web Development. I always had an interest in how websites/applications worked since the MySpace days and their customization. However, it never occurred to me to make it my passion and career. I was a business major in college because I enjoy creating things, problem solving, and seeing ideas come to fruition. The inkling to code and curiosity for web development grew tremendously while reading about it in college. Eventually my passion to code won over and I decided that I want to create things with code. I joined General Assembly and instantly felt captivated by code. Finding ways for people to connect, socialize, and have unique experiences through digital applications and code is my passion.
